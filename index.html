Hello, My name is John Bowser

I'm a software developer located in Salt Lake City, UT.

As a former Phlebotomist, Quality Assurance Lead, and Carpenter; I'm interested in:

  • Quick and painless web and app designs
  • Attention to detail and high quality standards
  • Well constructed code that is easy to read and work with

About John

After graduating with a B.S. Biology degree from Indiana University in 2013 I took a position as a phlebotomist at BioLife Plasma Services. After nearly 6 years there, having held roles such as Quality Assurance Lead and Center Supervisor, I realized I wanted to work in an industry that allowed me to build things. I spent a year as an Apprentice Carpenter and enjoyed all the new skills I was learning. At that time I realized that I could combine my desire to create things with my strong sense of quality and my passion for all things tech into my dream job as a Software Developer.

As a new student at Thinkful I am already learning so many new and interesting things. I'm excited to be working, growing, and learning with my classmates and mentors! My skills are rapidly developing and soon I will be a full-stack developer, creating dynamic and professional webpages and applications.

When I'm not digging into coding tutorials on my computer or in a book I can usually be found in the mountains or deserts of Utah with my girlfriend and my camera.
